Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

L10 AI Labs ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal data of our users, customers, and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, process, and protect information when you use our services.

This Privacy Policy applies to:

  • Our website
  • Our WhatsApp automation and messaging services
  • Any software, integrations, or tools operated by L10 AI Labs

By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

Legal name: L10 AI Labs

Location: India

Role: Data Controller for platform and customer data

L10 AI Labs provides AI-powered automation tools that help businesses manage customer conversations on WhatsApp using the WhatsApp Business Platform.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum data required to operate our services.

2.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Name
  • Business name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • WhatsApp Business Account details
  • Messages sent to or received through WhatsApp automation
  • Support communications

2.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Log files and timestamps
  • API usage data
  • Webhook events from Meta (WhatsApp)

2.3 Platform Data from Meta

When you connect your WhatsApp Business Account via Meta's Embedded Signup, we may receive:

  • WhatsApp Business Account ID (WABA ID)
  • Phone Number ID
  • Message metadata (sender number, timestamps)
  • Message content (only to process automation)

We do not access personal Facebook profiles beyond what is required for business onboarding.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use data only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Enabling WhatsApp messaging automation
  • Responding to user-initiated messages
  • Managing message templates
  • Providing customer support
  • Monitoring system performance and security
  • Complying with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Improving our services

We do not use your data for:

  • Selling to third parties
  • Advertising unrelated products
  • Profiling users for marketing without consent

4. WhatsApp Messaging & Opt-In

Businesses using our platform are responsible for:

  • Obtaining valid user opt-in before messaging
  • Respecting opt-out requests
  • Following WhatsApp's 24-hour messaging window rules
  • Using approved message templates where required

L10 AI Labs acts as a technical service provider and does not initiate unsolicited messages on behalf of businesses.

5. Data Processors & Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted service providers who process data on our behalf, including:

  • Cloud hosting providers (servers and infrastructure)
  • n8n (workflow automation)
  • Meta Platforms, Inc. (WhatsApp Cloud API)

All processors are contractually required to protect data and use it only as instructed.

6. Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary:

  • Message data is processed in real time and not permanently stored unless required for service operation or troubleshooting
  • Logs may be retained for security and compliance
  • Clients may request deletion of their data at any time

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures, including:

  • Secure API access
  • Token-based authentication
  • Encrypted communication
  • Restricted access controls
  • Regular system monitoring

However, no system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may disclose data only if:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • Necessary to comply with regulatory obligations
  • Required to protect our rights, users, or systems

9. International Data Transfers

Our services may involve processing data on servers located outside India. When this occurs, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data.

10. User Rights

Depending on applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Access their data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion
  • Withdraw consent
  • Object to certain processing

Requests can be made by contacting us using the details below.

11. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

12. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use basic cookies or analytics tools to improve functionality and performance. You can control cookie settings through your browser.

13. Third-Party Links

Our website or services may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

14. Compliance with Laws

We comply with:

  • WhatsApp Business Terms of Service
  • Meta Platform Policies
  • Indian Information Technology Act, 2000
  • Applicable data protection laws

Businesses using our platform are responsible for ensuring their own legal compliance.

15. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of our services indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
